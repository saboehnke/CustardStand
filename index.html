Our Story

A Southern Illinois Legend

The Custard Stand isn't just an ice cream shop — it's a Sesser institution. For over seven decades, generations of families have gathered at the corner of Route 148 and Route 154 to share something sweet. That tradition lives on every single day.

👨‍👦
Marion & Mark Lappin Co-Owners · Lappin Family Since 1986
🏠
1949
Cliff Robinson opens the original stand at the corner of Rts. 148 & 154 in Sesser, IL. The Dream Cone recipe is born.
🔑
1986
Barbara Lappin purchases the stand, renaming it from "Ye Olde Dairy Barn" and continuing the Dream Cone legacy.
👨‍👦
Today
Brothers Marion & Mark Lappin carry on the family tradition as co-owners, serving Southern Illinois's most beloved custard stand.
